Josh Spring

CEO and Co-Founder

Josh is a lifelong Alaskan and veteran political operative with experience on over 20 winning campaigns. Josh was instrumental in securing a bipartisan majority in the Alaska state house in 2016, the 2017 progressive sweep of the Anchorage Assembly, as well as Ethan Berkowitz's 2015 Anchorage mayoral election. His five years of experience as an assembly aide have given him unique insights into the concerns of Anchorage voters. Also a certified paralegal, Josh brings a valuable set of experience and skills to his campaigns.

Paul Oliva

Founder and Lead Developer

Paul studied Computer Systems Engineering at the University of Alaska-Anchorage before dropping out to take a job at a Silicon Valley startup. When he founded DataShark with Joshua Spring in May 2017, he told his California boss he'd be working remotely from now on, loaded up his van, and headed back to Alaska.
